Simply Blinded Biography:


Officially formed in 2023, Simply Blinded, A south texas Emo/Pop punk outfit with 4 members began its musical journey.


Simply Blinded has explored most of their home state, Texas, touring big cities such as: Austin, Houston and Dallas. They have performed local in the 956 and are originally from Harlingen, Tx.
